About Me

Bridging biomedical engineering with AI for transformative healthcare solutions

Professional Summary

Experienced biomedical engineer, digital health research scientist, consultant, and educator with over a decade of success developing and deploying innovative algorithms for the analysis and deployment of complex, real-world biomedical and behavioral signals collected using medical devices, wearable technology, and smartphones. Led the end-to-end design of scalable ML systems and advanced signal processing methods for passively collected, multivariate, multimodal medical data. Filed patents on time-frequency algorithms enabling early detection and classification in longitudinal health monitoring. Consulted for leading academic and healthcare institutions, translating cutting-edge research into practical solutions. Published research articles in top conferences and journals, and consistently contributed to high-impact cross-disciplinary clinical research projects at the interface of signal processing, AI, and mental health.
